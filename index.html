Meet My Furry Family

Willow

Hi, my name is Willow the Akita. My daddy adopted me 6 years ago from Southern Akita Rescue Alliance. My previous parents moved out of their home and had to leave me behind. I was alone tied to a tree for three days until SARA came and rescued me. Since being with my new Daddy, I have toys, cuddles, and all the peanut butter I can eat. It's not a bad life, but I wish I had a little sibling to play with.

Bjorn

Hi, my name is Bjorn the cat. My daddies adopted me 3 years ago from a local shelter. My daddies came in just to look at the cats. When we first met I was so tired and weak from my neutering surgery that I fell asleep in their arms. From the moment they held me, I knew I was safe and loved. When we got home, I got to meet my new sister Willow. The medication from my surgery wore off and I felt much better. I then began to run around the house and climb all over my cat tree and my daddies.

Furry Family

Now, we have a forever family. We feel loved and safe. My sister and I take naps on the regular and play together.
